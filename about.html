VD Gulf is a leading independent MRO based near Sharjah International Airport, Sharjah, UAE. In 2013 VD Gulf built a large hangar of approximately 22 000 square meter area, designed to facilitate large aircraft maintenance and repair. The hangar is capable of accommodating six narrow body (737/A320 size) and two wide body (747 size) aircraft simultaneously. It is complemented with all required special docking, tools/ equipment and utilities appropriate for efficient and quality maintenance of various types of aircraft/engine combination. The hangar is fully air-conditioned and have a high quality fire detection and protection system.

History

1996

Opening Volga-Dnepr Airlines line maintenance station in Sharjah, UAE

2013

Opening Volga-Dnepr Gulf 22.000 sq.m. Hangar in Sharjah, UAE

2014

Volga-Dnepr Gulf was awarded EASA approval for line and base maintenance on B747-400, B737CL/NG and line maintenance on A320 family.

2015

Volga Dnepr Gulf (VD Gulf) was awarded an extended EASA Part 145 Maintenance Organization approval for Unlimited Maintenance capability on Boeing 747-400 and Boeing 737CL/NG aircraft models.

2016

Unlimited EASA approval including component OH. Completed first 737CL “D” Check. Started a line of 747-400 heavy checks.
